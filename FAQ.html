    Who Are We?

    Xavier's Delightful Treats is a family-owned business located in Manlapay, Dumanjug, Cebu. With a passion for crafting customized cakes, we bring a personal touch to every creation. Our family is dedicated to delivering quality and excellence in every cake we make, ensuring that your celebrations are filled with joy and delicious memories. Choose Xavier's Delightful Treats for a taste of homemade goodness and heartfelt service from our family to yours.

    Do We Deliver?

    Yes, we offer delivery services for our customized cakes. We ensure that your delicious treats are delivered safely and on time to your desired location. Let us take care of bringing joy to your celebrations with our convenient delivery service. Charge may apply depends on your location.

    How To Order?

    Step 1: Brainstorm - Discuss your cake preferences, design ideas, and any special requests with us.

    Step 2: Fill up the order form - Provide details such as cake flavor, size, decoration, delivery preferences, and contact information in our easy-to-use order form.

    Step 3: Enjoy - Sit back, relax, and savor the delightful experience as we craft and deliver your customized cake, ensuring it's a highlight of your celebration.

    What Is Tier Level?

    Tier 1: - A Tier 1 cake typically refers to the first or bottom tier of a multi-tiered cake.

    Tier 2: - Tier two refers to the second tier or layer of a multi-tiered cake. In a tiered cake, each tier is stacked on top of another.

    Tier 3: - Tier 3 is placed on top of Tier 2, which is itself positioned above Tier 1 (the bottom tier).
